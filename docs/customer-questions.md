# Customer Discovery Interview Guide

## Introduction

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?

2. Your participation is voluntary, and your responses will remain anonymous. Is it okay if we take notes?

---

## Experience

3. Can you tell us about the most recent time you lost an item or had difficulty finding one on campus?

4. Could you walk us through what you did after you realized the item was missing?

5. Which part of the process was the most difficult or frustrating for you?

---

## Current Solution

6. How do you usually try to recover lost items on campus?

7. What people, places, or tools do you rely on?
   *(e.g., friends, security office, social media, university staff)*

8. What do you think could be improved about the current process?

---

## Problem Validation

9. How often do you or your friends experience this situation?

10. On a scale of **1–5**, how serious do you think this problem is? Why?

11. What happens if the lost item is not recovered?

---

## Closing

12. If you could change one thing about the current lost-and-found process, what would it be?

13. Is there anything else you'd like to share about this issue?

14. May we use your anonymous responses as part of our ICT105 course project?

---

## Participant Information

- Participation is voluntary.
- You may skip any question or stop the interview at any time.
- Your responses will remain anonymous.
